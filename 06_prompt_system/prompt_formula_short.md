# Short Prompt Formula

Use this formula when you want a quick prompt.

```text
[SUBJECT] made of [LACE FAMILY] with [MOTIF TYPE], [GROUND TYPE], [EDGE TYPE], visible thread paths, active negative space, transparent holes, [MATERIAL], [LIGHTING]
```

## Examples

```text
moon moth made of Chantilly lace with floral sprigs, fine net ground, scalloped picot edge, visible black thread paths, transparent holes, silk thread, soft backlight
```

```text
radial doily mandala in Irish crochet lace, raised roses and leaves joined by irregular mesh, cotton thread, tiny shadows on dark velvet
```
