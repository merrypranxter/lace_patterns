# Glossary

- **Allover**: continuous lace fabric field.
- **Bar / bride**: bridge connecting motifs.
- **Bobbin lace**: lace made by crossing and twisting bobbin-held threads.
- **Corded lace**: lace with heavier outline thread.
- **Cutwork**: fabric cut away and stitched.
- **Doily**: small ornamental mat, often radial crochet or tatting.
- **Filet**: square grid lace with filled/open cells.
- **Ground**: base net or mesh supporting motifs.
- **Guipure**: motif lace connected by bars, usually no net.
- **Insertion**: strip placed between fabric pieces.
- **Needle lace**: lace made with needle and thread over a pattern.
- **Picot**: tiny loop or knot decoration.
- **Scallop**: rounded repeating edge.
- **Tatting**: knotted ring and chain lace.
- **Void**: open hole; active negative space.
