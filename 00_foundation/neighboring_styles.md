# Neighboring Styles

## Lace vs embroidery

Embroidery decorates a ground fabric. Lace creates openwork structure.

## Lace vs mesh

Mesh is usually repeated open grid. Lace includes decorative motif hierarchy.

## Lace vs crochet

Crochet is a method. Crochet lace is one kind of lace, not all lace.

## Lace vs macrame

Macrame is knotting with heavier cord. Lace is usually finer and more textile-like.

## Lace vs filigree

Filigree is metal ornament. Lace can resemble filigree, but it is thread-based.
