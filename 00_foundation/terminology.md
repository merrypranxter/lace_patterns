# Lace Terminology

## Core terms

- **Ground**: the base net or mesh that holds motifs.
- **Motif**: decorative unit such as flower, leaf, scroll, medallion, star, shell, fan, diamond.
- **Bride / bar**: thread bridge connecting elements in needle lace or guipure.
- **Picot**: tiny loop, knot, or nub along an edge or bar.
- **Scallop**: rounded repeating edge shape.
- **Insertion**: strip of lace inserted between fabric pieces.
- **Edging**: lace used along an edge.
- **Allover**: repeating lace across a field.
- **Medallion**: centralized motif unit.
- **Sprig**: small floral motif.
- **Cord / gimp**: heavier outline thread.
- **Mesh**: repeated ground holes.
- **Net**: open foundation with repeated cells.
- **Bars**: openwork connectors.
- **Fillings**: decorative stitches inside motif areas.
