# Material And Scale

Lace changes drastically depending on thread, yarn, fiber, and scale.

## Fine lace

Delicate thread, tiny holes, high density, often bridal, heirloom, veil, lingerie, collar, cuff, insertion.

## Medium lace

Visible motifs, readable thread paths, useful for apparel, trims, overlays, and table linens.

## Heavy lace

Corded, crochet-like, guipure-like, suitable for graphic overlays, apparel, decor, and laser-cut translation.

## Materials

- cotton: matte, clean, craft and heritage feel
- linen: crisp, old-world, structural
- silk: soft sheen, luxury, delicate
- nylon/polyester: machine lace, lingerie, stretch, modern
- metallic thread: ornate, ceremonial, costume, stage
- wool: useful in knitted lace and shawls
